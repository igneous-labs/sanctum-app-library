Here's what we shipped last week:

## Seeker Season is Live!!!

![image of seeker season banner](https://sanctum.so/_next/image?url=%2Fblog%2Fimage-3.png&w=1200&q=100&dpl=dpl_6FYeqkc4tv6HEbgYzJbYUJQSMU3V)

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/igneous-labs/sanctum-app-library/main/src/assets/seeker_season_screenshots.png)

Seeker Season is here! Seeker Season is a special early-access adventure just for Solana Seeker users. As you deposit SOL, you'll level up Albus and unlock new cities as backgrounds in the app. These backgrounds are exclusive to this Seeker Season, so grab them while you can! Explore the world with Albus～

Seeker Season will run until 18th of May. We're looking to evolve and improve Seeker Season so let us know what you'd like to see!

## **A new and improved Library**

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/igneous-labs/sanctum-app-library/main/src/assets/library_screenshot.jpg)

The Library (the section of the app you're reading this in) got a significant upgrade this week. Content is now organized into sections, there are new articles to read, and we've published two deep dives: one on how Daily Rewards work, and one on the Library itself. If you've ever had questions about how the app works, this is the place to start.

Oh, and you can expect one of these "What We Shipped" posts every week from now on!

## A more enjoyable rewards receipt screen

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/igneous-labs/sanctum-app-library/main/src/assets/rewards_earned_banner.png)

Every day, the app pays out your rewards. The moment you claim them should make you feel good, and the old approach didn't quite do it justice. We've therefore implemented a new juicy rewards screen that should hopefully fire up your dopamine circuits! Let us know what you think.

## Over-the-air updates

Behind the scenes, we've shipped something that'll make your life easier without you ever noticing: over-the-air (OTA) update support. When we push an improvement or fix, it lands on your device automatically the next time you open the app.

(You may need to refresh the app once or twice to trigger the update once the update is installed onto your device. We're working on better notifications to inform you of this fact.)

## Bugfixes

A few users have been unable to log in and/or to view their balance.
We've identified the root cause to a connection problem with one of our infrastructure providers. All balances are safe, but users were panicking when they could not see their balance due to that. We pushed a fix to that problem and this week we're continuing to resolve this problem further to make it much less scary.
